Private Land Access for Families Who Want More Than a Weekend Escape

Kinscape is a veteran-owned membership unlocking hand-selected private lands across the U.S. for families who crave meaningful outdoor experiences, sustainable recreation, and freedom to roam — without the noise, crowds, or permit restrictions.

  • Explore secluded forests, rivers, and other beautiful landscapes
  • Camp, hike, fish, and reconnect with your kids in pristine nature
  • Access land 365 days a year — on your schedule, your terms
  • Support regenerative landowners and long-term conservation
